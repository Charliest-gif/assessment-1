5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Exercise

How to Use This Exercise

Take a slow breath. Work through each card one step at a time, using your senses to notice the world around you.

5 things you can see

Let your eyes wander and pick out colors, shapes, or details you don’t usually pay attention to.

4 things you can feel

It could be the texture of your clothes, the ground under your feet, or even the temperature of the air.

3 things you can hear

Some may be close, like your own breathing, and others might be farther away, like traffic or birds.

2 things you can smell

If nothing stands out, take a slow breath and recall a smell you enjoy, like coffee or a favorite candle.

1 thing you can taste

Maybe you can taste that sip of water, a mint, or just the natural taste in your mouth.
