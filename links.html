Other Useful Contacts

Samaritans

www.samaritans.org | 116 123 | jo@samaritans.org
Available 24/7, offering confidential emotional support to anyone feeling distressed, overwhelmed, or having suicidal thoughts.

Mind

www.mind.org.uk | To call 0300 123 3393, or text 86463 86463 | info@mind.org.uk
Mind offers advice, support and information to people experiencing a mental health difficulty and their family and friends. Lines are open Monday to Friday 9am to 6pm (except bank holidays).

Campaign Against Living Miserably (CALM)

www.thecalmzone.net | 0800 58 58 58 | www.thecalmzone.net/help/webchat/ (Webchat)
A helpline for people in the UK who are down or have hit a wall for any reason, who need to talk or find information and support. Open 5pm to midnight, every day of the year.
